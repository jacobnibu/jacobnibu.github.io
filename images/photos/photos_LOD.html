Random Pictures of South India

2015-04-21

South India has some interesting places that are less known to the world outside. The two States at the southern end of Indian subcontinent, Tamilnadu and Kerala, have lush green mountains and plains nurtured by the monsoon every year.

This is a small sample of photographs from random places in South India.
